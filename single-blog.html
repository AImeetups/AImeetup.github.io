Hiring Volunteers Blogger

January 14, 2019 Kourosh Hashemi

It is hard to imagine the Internet without blogs. Whether it’s on Tumblr, WordPress, or some site you cooked up yourself, a blog is a great way to share your thoughts with the world. You might write about a person you admire, a movie you hate, an event that changed your life, or just some food that was really good.

we believe that volunteering is society’s most powerful tool to solve problems and help those in need. At the same time, social organisations struggle to keep up with our rapidly evolving digital society and keep in touch with potential volunteers.
